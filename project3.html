Create a New Species

Tool(s): Photoshop

Project Description: Using photoshop, I blended images of a baby polar bear, a baby penguin, and deer to create a new animal species. I blended the bear head with the body of the penguin, and tried to have the white blend into the grey body. I added deer antlers on its head and blended a deer pattern on its belly. For the background, I played around with Photoshop's AI tool for the first time and I liked the result! The AI tool really helped me capture an icy ocean and glacier environment- a perfect atmosphere for an Antarctic new species.

A new species- baby penguin + polar bear + deer
