Glitch Art Collage

Tool(s): Photoshop, TextEdit

Project Description: I created this glitch collage by starting off with an image of jellyfish. I wanted to play around with an underwater scheme- some blues and the vibrant colors of white and orange of the jellyfish. Using TextEdit, I was able to tweak and glitch the image. I used photoshop to organize the glitched jellyfish into rows. I kept the original image of the jellyfish in the background to contrast my glitched result.
